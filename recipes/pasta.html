Cheesy Chicken and Bacon Pasta Bake

Description

This baked chicken and bacon pasta in creamy sauce is a weeknight meal that's simple to put together and can be customized. Want it to be spicy? Swap out the seasoning. Want a different pasta? It can be swapped out, too.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces penne pasta
  • 3 ounces pork shoulder bacon, cut into strips
  • 12 ounces chicken breast meat, cubed
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 4 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 ounces shredded Gruyere cheese
